What we supply

Two materials. One reliable source.

We keep our range focused so every order gets full attention — deformed bar for structural work, and cement for every stage of the pour.

01 / Structural Steel

Deformed Bar (Rebar)

Ribbed reinforcement bar for slabs, columns, beams, and foundations — manufactured and distributed to the specifications your project calls for.

  • 8–32mm diameter
  • Ribbed / deformed profile
  • Bulk & retail volumes
  • Site delivery available

Deformed bar gets its grip from ribs rolled into the surface, which bond the steel to concrete far better than a smooth rod would — that's what lets a slab or column carry tension without cracking loose. Not sure which diameter your drawings call for? Use the weight calculator below to check quantities before you call in an order.

02 / Foundation Material

Cement

Supply and distribution of cement for foundations, plastering, block work, and finishing — sourced and delivered to match your project timeline.

  • Bag & bulk supply

  • Consistent stock availability
